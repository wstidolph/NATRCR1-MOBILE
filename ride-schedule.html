What is CTR? Competitive Trail Riding is a timed event where horse and rider teams navigate trails while being judged on the horse's condition, soundness, and manners, as well as the rider's horsemanship.

Divisions: Open (O), Competitive Pleasure (CP), Novice (N), and Leisure (L) - there's a division for every skill level!
